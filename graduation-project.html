Graduation Project

Design and Implementation of an Autonomous RC Car Based on Computer Vision and Deep Learning

For Simulation, we Used OpenCV for Image Preprocessing & NVIDIA CNN Model with Keras Library for model training, following the impeccable tutoring of The Complete Self-Driving Car Course on Udemy.

In addition, we deployed a successful hardware model of the Donkey Car Open-Source Project.

Donkey Car Is A Self-Driving Car Platform, Simulator and Python Library for High-Level Self-Driving Hobby RC Cars.

The initial system components were to choose a 1/10 scale RC Electric Car (Kyosho DMT VE-R) with a 3S LiPo Battery and a 2400KV Brushless Motor. Along the capturing power of Raspberry Pi Camera V2, and Computation Power of the NVIDIA Jetson Nano 4 GB B01 Developer Kit.

We managed to earn a Competition Achievement Award from IC-SIT 2023 Competition Organized by IEEE Egypt AP-S / MTT-S Joint Chapter and SCGRS Technological Company Golden Sponsorship.
